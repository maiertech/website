## Transcript

### The symptoms

- https://sveltejobs.com/
- https://sveltesociety.dev/job
- If you are lucky, it's mentioned in a list of frameworks, meaning no harm done if you love Svelte.
- On the Svelte Discord there are many dubious job offers, some obviously underpaying. And if there
  is a real one, the poster will get flooded with applications.

### Who uses Svelte?

- Mature companies, meaning such that can provide a certain level of job security, made their
  framework choices years ago.
- Greenfield project can be an opportunity to advocate for Svelte, but don't ask your employer to
  reqrite a mature codebase (There are exceptions to the rule:
  https://strawberrybrowser.com/blog/react-to-svelte)
- Freelancers use Svelte when the customer does not care about the framework.
- Less mature companies and agenices use Svelte, too, but the downside are mediocre pay and job
  security.

  ### Conclusion

  Don't expect miractles when it comes to Svelte adoption. Your best bet to work with Svelte is to
  seize an opportunity at your current employer if the stars align.

  You should be proficient in one of the other big frameworks. Which one dependes on the job market
  you are targeting. Here in the Netherlands it's Ract and Vue and a little bit of Angular.

  Don't underestimate the value of the ecosystems. Vue has built a very strong ecosystem and value
  proposition for companies.
